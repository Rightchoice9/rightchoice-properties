Strategic Lettings

Guaranteed rent, company lets, supported living and long-term lease solutions.

We help landlords explore alternative letting routes that can reduce voids, create stability and improve long-term asset performance.

300+ landlords assisted

Not every property should be let the same way.

Some homes, HMOs, blocks and larger properties may be better suited to company lets, supported living providers, care providers or FRI lease structures. We help landlords assess suitability and connect with appropriate routes.

Guaranteed Rent

Explore guaranteed rent style agreements where suitable, designed to create predictable income and reduce day-to-day involvement.

Company Lets

Lease options with companies and operators who require accommodation for staff, contractors or housing operations.

Supported Living

Helping landlords explore whether their property may suit supported accommodation providers.

Care Provider Leases

Suitable larger properties may appeal to care providers requiring long-term accommodation solutions.

FRI Leases

Full repairing and insuring lease structures can offer landlords a more hands-off long-term arrangement.

Block Leasing

For landlords with multiple units or blocks, we can explore lease options with operators and providers.
