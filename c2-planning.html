C2 planning & care sector opportunities

Unlock the potential of your property through C2 planning.

Helping landlords explore care sector opportunities, supported accommodation routes and long-term provider lease options for suitable properties.

What is C2?

Specialist accommodation can create a different route for landlords.

C2 planning use relates to residential institutions and can include certain care-related accommodation settings. For suitable properties, exploring a C2 route may create opportunities for higher income potential, longer-term occupancy and partnerships with professional care providers.

Not every property will be suitable, but for the right property in the right location, a care-related strategy can offer a more stable alternative to traditional residential letting.

Suitable properties

What type of property could be considered?

Suitability depends on planning position, layout, local demand, property condition and provider requirements.

Larger family homes

Spacious homes with multiple bedrooms, good communal areas and suitable access may sometimes be considered for specialist accommodation routes.

HMOs or former HMOs

Some HMOs or multi-bedroom properties may be suitable for review where layout, licensing and planning considerations align.

Detached or semi-detached properties

Properties with strong layouts, parking, multiple bathrooms or adaptation potential may be attractive to certain providers.

Questions landlords ask

C2 planning FAQs.

Can every property become C2?

No. Suitability depends on the property, planning considerations, layout, location, local authority position and provider demand.

Do you guarantee planning approval?

No. Planning outcomes cannot be guaranteed. We can help assess the opportunity and guide landlords on what may need reviewing.

Can you introduce care providers?

Yes, where a property is suitable and there is relevant demand, we can introduce opportunities to care providers and supported living operators.
