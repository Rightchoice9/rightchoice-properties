Landlord Services

Practical lettings and management services for landlords who want their property rented, managed and maintained with clarity.

Core services

Choose the level of support that fits your property.

Some landlords only need help finding a tenant. Others want rent collected or a fully managed service. We offer a flexible service structure so you can choose the level of involvement that suits you.

Below is a clearer breakdown of what each service can include, so landlords can understand the difference between tenant find, rent collection and fully managed support.

Fully Managed

Fully Managed Service

Designed for landlords who want a hands-off approach to property ownership, while still having clear oversight of rent, tenants, maintenance and tenancy matters.

Rental valuation
Property marketing
Accompanied viewings
Tenant referencing
Right to Rent checks
Tenancy agreement preparation
Deposit registration support
Rent collection
Arrears monitoring
Tenant communication
Maintenance coordination
Contractor management
Property inspection support
Lease renewals
Rent reviews
End of tenancy support
Deposit dispute assistance
Landlord reporting

Ideal for

Busy landlords
Portfolio landlords
Overseas landlords
Hands-off investors

Tenant Find Service
